How we met

It all began in the summer of 2018, in the vibrant city of Guadalajara, Mexico. Ayda and I were both part of a program called PEPE, where we took Spanish and cultural classes. As the program came to an end, Ayda gave me a letter on the beach she wrote in Portuguese describing how wonderful and unique she thought I was and stated that if I ever visited São Paulo Brazil, she would be there to show me her world. We kept in touch, and when I traveled there a year later, Ayda became my girlfriend. From that moment on, our story truly began—a journey filled with love, laughter, and countless memories across borders. Now, we are excited to write the next chapter of our story.
